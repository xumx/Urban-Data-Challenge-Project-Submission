'A Day in Geneva' uses CartoDB to transform transportation dataset into a datacube format, which allows us to render big, timeseries data in the client.

Special thanks to CartoDB for supporting us with their super awesome GIS backend. This project wouldn't be possible without their platform.

Browser Support:

Feature 1: Feel the pulse

A dynamic and interactive visualization of the public transportation network in Geneva.
Use the controls on the top right to adjust the playback speed and explore the timeseries.

Feature 2: See the impact

A 3D visualization of the cumulative traffic volume in each tram stop.
Red lines represent a station with more citizens alighting than boarding.
Blue lines represent a station with more people boarding the public transport.

Feature 3:

Plot selected routes and station names on the map. Followed by a Google Earth simulation of the tram route.
