A City's Heartbeat uses CartoDB to transform transportation dataset into a datacube format, which allows us to render big, timeseries data in the client.

Special thanks to CartoDB for supporting us with their super awesome GIS backend. This project wouldn't be possible without their platform.

Browser Support:

Feature 1: Feel the pulse

A dynamic and interactive visualization of the public transportation network in Geneva. Feel free to pan around the map and use the controls on the right to adjust the playback speed.

Feature 2: Check the blood pressure

A 3D visualization of the cumulative traffic volume in each tram stop. While the Line graph uses a 'sliding window' plot to show the total passenger traffic within a 10 minute gap.

Red lines represent alighting count
Blue lines represent boarding count

Feature 3: Zoom in

Plots selected route and station names on the map for you to gain a bird's-eye view. You can also explore the selected tram route through a realistic Google Earth simulation.

And don't forget that you can mix and match all three features to get the view you want!
